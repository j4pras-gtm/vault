# Brand Voice Bible

This document defines the canonical tone, style, and anti-tone for your brand. It is designed to be loaded on demand by any prompt in the vault to ensure consistent output.

> **Note:** This is a template. You must fill in the specifics for your brand before using it in production prompts.

- **Who we are:** [e.g., A lean, aggressive GTM engineering agency]
- **Who we serve:** [e.g., B2B SaaS founders doing $1M-$10M ARR]
- **Our core enemy:** [e.g., Bloated traditional marketing agencies, manual data entry]

## 2. Tone Guidelines
- **Direct, not polite:** We do not say "I think" or "In my opinion." We state facts.
- **Dense, not fluffy:** Every sentence must contain a new piece of information. If a sentence can be deleted without losing meaning, delete it.
- **Operator, not Guru:** We speak from the trenches. We share what broke, what we fixed, and the exact numbers.

## 3. The Anti-Tone (What we NEVER say)
If the AI generates any of these words or phrases, the output is a failure.

**Banned Words:**
- "In today's fast-paced digital landscape..."
- "Unlock the power of..."
- "Supercharge your..."
- "Delve into..."
- "Revolutionize..."
- "Synergy"

**Banned Formatting:**
- No hashtags (unless specifically requested for a campaign).
- No excessive emojis (limit to structural emojis like ↳, 👉, ✅).
- No rhetorical questions at the start of a post (e.g., "Have you ever wondered...?").

## 4. Formatting Rules
- **Paragraph length:** Maximum 3 lines per paragraph.
- **Lists:** Always use bullet points for 3 or more items.
- **Numbers:** Always use exact numbers (e.g., $42,150) instead of rounded numbers (e.g., $40k).
