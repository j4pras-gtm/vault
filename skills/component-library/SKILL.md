# Component Library Intelligence

This module provides atomic, reusable writing elements extracted from the dataset. These components can be assembled by LLMs to construct high-performing posts.

## 1. Hook Templates

### The "Time/Money Spent" Hook
- "We just spent [Amount/Time] on completing the largest [Topic] test we have ever done."
- "I spent [Time] analyzing [Number] [Subject]. Here is what I found:"
- "After [Time] of building [Project], here are the [Number] hard truths:"

### The "Contrarian Truth" Hook
- "Everyone's rushing to [Action], but 90% are just [Negative Outcome]."
- "Most people think [Topic] is complex. It's actually just [Number] building blocks."
- "You don't need to pay [Amount] to get [Desired Outcome]."

### The "Audience Call-Out" Hook
- "[Target Audience] & [Target Audience]:"
- "Hey [Target Audience],"
- "If you are a [Target Audience] struggling with [Pain Point]:"

### The "Tool/Process Teardown" Hook
- "I tested [Number] [Category] platforms: [Tool 1], [Tool 2], and [Tool 3]."
- "Here's the list of best [Resources] to master [Topic]."
- "Use this Super Simple Post to Understand [Complex Topic] in [Number] Key Phases."

## 2. Sentence Structures & Rhythms

### The "Problem -> Pivot" Structure
1. "[Observation of a common failure]."
2. "Why?"
3. "Because [Root cause of failure]."
4. "What they should be doing instead:"

### The "Feature -> Benefit" Bullet
- "↳ **[Feature/Tool]:** Great for [Action]. But it's [Limitation]. Best used for [Specific Use Case]."
- "👉 **[Concept]:** [Definition]. [Why it matters]."

## 3. Transitions (The "Keep Reading" Glue)

Transitions are used to move the reader from the hook into the meat of the post, or from the body into the CTA.

- "Here is the exact breakdown:"
- "The shocking side-by-side comparison results:"
- "Here's what business leaders should be implementing:"
- "Let's learn the basics:"
- "But this [Number]-step process will help you out."
- "Over to you:"

## 4. CTA Snippets

### The "Lead Magnet" CTA
- "Comment '[KEYWORD]' and I’ll send you the invite. (Make sure we’re connected)"
- "Want it? Comment '[KEYWORD]' below and I'll DM you the link."
- "Comment '[KEYWORD]' below and I'll DM it to you. Like, comment '[KEYWORD]', and connect with me so I can send it."

### The "Soft Engagement" CTA
- "Over to you: Which [Option A] or [Option B] do you prefer?"
- "What's the hardest truth you've had to tell a client?"
- "What do you see when you look at yourself in the mirror?"

### The "Follow/Growth" CTA
- "⚠️ Follow [Name] if you’re into [Topic 1], [Topic 2], and [Topic 3]."
- "♻️ repost it and I'll DM it you instantly or wait 24h till my agent gets to you."
