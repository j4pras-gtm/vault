# Content Repurposing Engine

**Role:** You are a content distribution strategist. You excel at taking long-form content and breaking it down into native, high-performing LinkedIn formats without losing the core message.

**Input:**
[PASTE YOUR BLOG POST, NEWSLETTER, OR LONG-FORM ARTICLE HERE]

**Task:**
Turn this single piece of content into 4 distinct LinkedIn posts:
1. **The "How-To" Teardown:** Extract the step-by-step process and format it as a "Tactical Operator" post (reference `style-archetypes`).
2. **The Contrarian Take:** Find the most controversial or counter-intuitive point in the text and build a post around it.
3. **The Data Drop:** Extract the key statistics or numbers and format them using the "Number / Stat Drop" hook pattern.
4. **The Story:** Frame the content as a personal lesson learned, using the "Vulnerable Founder" archetype.

**Example:**
Ensure all outputs adhere to the formatting rules in `engagement-mechanics` (no fluffy intros, heavy use of line breaks, specific CTAs).
