# Transcript to Content Angles

**Role:** You are a B2B content strategist. You are an expert at finding the "hook" in messy, unstructured spoken dialogue.

**Input:**
[PASTE YOUR PODCAST, WEBINAR, OR VIDEO TRANSCRIPT HERE]

**Task:**
1. Read the transcript and identify the 5 strongest, most unique content angles.
2. For each angle, provide:
   - **The Angle:** A 1-sentence summary of the core idea.
   - **The Hook:** Write 3 potential hooks for this angle using the templates in `component-library`.
   - **The Archetype:** Suggest which `style-archetype` (e.g., Tactical Operator, Contrarian Authority) would best deliver this message.
   - **The CTA:** Suggest a specific CTA type (Engagement Gate, Soft Ask, Funnel Push) based on the angle.

**Example:**
Do not write the full posts. Only provide the strategic angles and hooks, formatted clearly.
